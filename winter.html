Winter Outdoor Plants

Camellias grow in many different shades and colors while most varieties can grow well in cold climates. Camellias can add a touch of color to any garden
Snowdrops are the perfect winter flower! Not only do they look stunning in any winter garden but they can also survive very low temperatures and even snowfall.
Holly bushes add color to the bleak winter landscape with their red dots that brighten up any garden. They also look exceptional, especially if there is snow.
Bergenias will certainly add all the color you may need in your garden. Bergenias come in many different colors like purple and bronze. Just make sure that they have enough water.

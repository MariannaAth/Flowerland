Summer Outdoor Plants!

Also known as "bellflowers", cambanulas are beautiful flowers that are either white, pink, purple or clear blue. They are bell-shaped and come in many different varietes. They can grow in full-sun or light shade!

Pontetillas are orange, pink, red or white-colored flowers. They need sun to grow and watering once or twice a week during extreme heat. They are also known as shrubby or bush cinquefoil

Dahlias are beautiful flowers. They can be multicolored, orange, pink, purple, red, white or yellow. They need full sun and watering 2 or 3 times a week.

Zinnias can be multicolored, orange, pink, purple, red, white and yellow. They need full sun and and need to be watered once a week.

Hydrangeas are able to grow in any sun condition (full sun, part sun or shadow).They can be blue, green, pink, purple, red or white. It would be ideal to water them every morning to prevent them from drying. Hydrangeas attract butterflies and will make your garden even prettier!

They are also known as candytuft. It needs full sun to grow and it must be watered often.It can be white, purple or pink.
